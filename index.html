About Collage

Established in 2010 as a Center for Excellence, R. N. G. Patel Institute of Technology [RNGPIT] is recognized by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi and Gujarat Technological University (GTU), Ahmadabad.The institute is situated near Isroli-Afwa in Bardoli taluka of Surat District, about 4 KMs away from Bardoli Town. The institute provides students all possible infrastructure and educational facilities such as neat and airy classrooms, tutorial rooms, fully equipped laboratories, language lab to empower them in communicative English, computer center, library, workshop, playground and canteen.

Eskra - The Innovative Spark

Eskra serves as a platform where we want to set the reader's sight on the innovative minds with prominent practical skills of the students. It creates a shared vision which acts as a beacon that illuminates our next generation.
